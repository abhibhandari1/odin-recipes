Tandoori Chicken

Delicious Indian style Tandoori Chicken

This is an authentic recipe for Tandoori chicken. Serve with long grain Basmati rice, cucumber salad and grilled veggies.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Basmati rice
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Score chicken
  2. Marinate with yogurt and spices
  3. Bake in Indian oven
  4. Cook rice in a cooker
  5. Eat
